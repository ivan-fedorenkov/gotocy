Welcome

Denis, welcome to GoToCy website!

As a registered user you now have a privilege to control all of your properties as well as your personal contact details. Let me briefly describe a couple of important actions you need to perform as your first steps in our system.

Step #1 - Specify your contact details

The first step is to specify contact details that visitors of your property's pages will be using to send their requests. The form will ask you to provide the following details:

  • Your name (mandatory field)
  • Contact email (mandatory field)
  • Contact phone
  • Languages that you are able to use

Type in whatever you think is reasonable. Note that you can access the form at any time by clicking on the "My Profile" link of the "Navigation" bar.

Specify Contacts

Step #2 - Add your first property object

The next step is to add your property object. Feel free to skip this step if you already have one (you can verify it here). The form will ask you to provide the following details about your property:

  • Title
  • Property type
  • Plot size (if applicable)
  • Covered area
  • Floor(s)
  • Number of bedrooms
  • Number of guests
  • Distance to sea
  • Text description of your property
  • Position on a map
  • Pictures of your property

Be patient and carefully fill all of the required fields!

You can add as many properties as you wish later. Just click on the "My Properties" link of the "Navigation" bar. You will be redirected to the properties management page. From there you will be able to access the form by clicking on the "Add New Property" link of the "Actions" bar.
